A one-day greening conference with 100 to 150 guests including industry professionals and current students, industry associations etc. Registration will be at 8am with speakers, meetings and workshops starting at 9am. Plan for a quick opening ceremony a welcoming tea and coffee, five to six hours of speakers, meetings and workshops, a light lunch to be offered between with tea breaks and then followed by a cocktail hour accompanied by the closing ceremony.


As attendees will purchase tickets to attend the event, we are looking to bring along several sponsors in line with the goal and practices of the Go Green organization.
